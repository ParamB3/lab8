5. SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY INTERPRETATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
There are massive impacts of deforestation throughout the world.These include indigenous communities, biodiversity, carbon emissions and long-term climate stability in general.
This problem can be greatly aided by identifying high-risk years.This allows governments and organizations to implement environmental protections.

6. RECOMMENDATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Analyse external variables that impact High Risk or High Severity years
- Support sustainable land-use policies.
- Increase transparency in supply chains.
- Force mega corporations to take on policies for environmental safety
On conclusion, there are many aspects of deforestation that are ruining our land.
In order to fix these, we have to implement the strategies that have were mentioned above.
